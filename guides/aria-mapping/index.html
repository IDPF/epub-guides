Overview

This document provides mappings from the properties defined in the EPUB Structural Semantics Vocabulary to equivalent roles defined in the Digital Publishing WAI-ARIA Module 1.0 and ARIA 1.1.

The W3C Digital Publishing Interest Group is seeking optimal alignment of the old Structural Semantics Vocabulary with the Digital Publishing Module, but it is not anticipated that roles will be created for every property (e.g., because of lack of real-world implementation, and as a new balance away from niche-use terms is sought out). This work is ongoing, and will be reflected in this document as new roles become available.

For authoring differences between the EPUB 3 epub:type attribute and the WAI-ARIA role attribute, see the EPUB Types to ARIA Roles Authoring Guide.
